UYC Leadership Conference

1/9/17

Thank you very much for everyone's participation in the UYC 2017 Leadership Conference!!! Special thanks to our guest speakers, who talked about managing stress and leadership! We also had talks about the importance of volunteering, cover letters and resumes, job interview tips as well as our special cardboard shelter activity! We hope you had something to take away from this experience! Thank you and let's work hard again next year!

Old Tyme Christmas

12/02/16

Thank you very much for everyone's participation in Old Tyme Christmas! We served free hot chocolate, popcorn and offered free public skating! It was a huge success and we raised $113 in donations for Covenant House! Your help is most appreciated and let's work hard again next year!
