About Us

We believe that the community deserves the best from the youth! UYC is a council that meet once a month and we ask that members attend and volunteer regularly. We promote the wellbeing of the community, and we encourage the youth to actively participate in the community by doing community events such as the Spring Carnival, Family Day Events, helping out with Covenant House, the 30 Hour Famine, Markham Idol, Olde Tyme Christmas, Tree Planting, Fun Fairs, Leadership Conferences, and so many more!
